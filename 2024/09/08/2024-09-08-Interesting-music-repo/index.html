Cadence

https://www.damvibes.com/music-theory/music-cadences/

Chord of resolution usually lies on th strong beat of the measure

PAC: Perfect Authetic cadence Ⅴ-Ⅰ(i)

V(7)−I(i)V^{(7)}-I(i)V(7)−I(i)

  1. Both Ⅴ and Ⅰ shuld be in root position
  2. The highest-sound note of tonic triad must be the tonic (I 的最高音是root音)

Strong sense of resolution, usually used for end of period and pieces.

IAC Imperfect authetic cadence Ⅴ-Ⅰ(i)

V(7)−I(i)V^{(7)}-I(i)V(7)−I(i)

  1. ≥1\ge 1≥1 is inverted
  2. DO NOT need: The highest-sound note of tonic triad must be the tonic

Weaker sense of resolution, usually used for end of period and pieces.

compound authentic cadence
Regardless of cadense is perfect, is VVV is precede by I46I_4^6I46​, the cadence is called compound authentic cadence

double cadence$
Authentic cadence can be embellished by suspending the leading tone/ suspension with cadential $I_4^6, have a double cadence

Half cadence (HC):Ⅰ(i), ii(ii∅ii^{\varnothing}ii∅), Ⅳ(iv) - Ⅴ

Ⅰ(i), ii(ii∅ii^{\varnothing}ii∅), Ⅳ(iv) - Ⅴ

Placed at the end of a phrase (group of 4 measures)

Plagal Cadence (Pl.C.): Ⅳ(iv) - Ⅰ(i)

Widely used in church music.

Deceptive cadence (DC) Ⅴ - Ⅲ(iii)

Technically, any progression in which I does not follow V can be considered a deceptive cadence
In practice, such labelling is reserved mainly for the progression V – vi

Voice leading: necessitates particular attention: the chord of resolution needs to have the third doubled.
